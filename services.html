The Process

What happens
to your bike

Every session follows the same careful sequence — no shortcuts, no rushing. We treat every bike like our own.

01
Pre-rinse & Inspection
We inspect the bike before touching it. Loose parts, fragile areas, existing scratches — all noted before we start.
02
pH-Neutral Foam Wash
Full foam application with our pH-neutral shampoo. Gentle enough for matte finishes, thorough enough to lift all grime.
03
Wax & Ceramic (package dependent)
Depending on your package, we apply hand-wax for gloss, spray-on Nano Ceramic Coating for UV protection, or both.
04
Dry & Final Check
Hand-dried, wiped down, and checked before we hand it back. You ride out clean — not wondering if we missed a spot.
